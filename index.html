How to Play

Uncover the corruption in four steps

Examine

Study each official's statement around the circular council table. Every role provides a unique piece of information.

Analyze

Determine who is telling the truth and who is lying. Corrupt officials always lie, but some innocents may be forced to lie too.

Cross-Reference

Use active abilities and hired professionals to verify your suspicions. Mark cards with notes and color ribbons to track your deductions.

Arrest

Arrest all corrupt officials to win. But be careful — three wrong arrests and you lose. And never arrest an innocent Mayor.

The Corrupt

Blackmailer
Special Effect

Forces adjacent innocent officials to lie permanently. They're still innocent — arresting them is a mistake.

Silencer
Special Effect

Silences one official at game start. They refuse to speak until the Silencer is arrested.

Converter
Special Effect

Converts one innocent official to the corrupt side at game start. The traitor hides among the clean.

Special Effect

Suppresses the Mayor's testimony. The Mayor trusts no one while the Manipulator lives.

Nepotist
Corrupt

No special power. Hired without merit, disguised as an innocent, and always lying.

Features

What makes Rotten Council unique

🔍

Pure Deduction

No luck, no randomness in gameplay. Every puzzle has a logical solution. Cross-reference statements, use abilities, and deduce the truth.

🏆

Campaign Mode

Progress through 30 levels across two acts. New roles and corrupt abilities unlock as you advance. One wrong move ends your run.

⚙

Custom Games

Set player count (5-15), corrupt count, difficulty, and choose which roles appear. Design your own puzzle challenge.

Hired Professionals

Deploy rare specialists — the Interrogator, Detective, and Janitor — as consumable abilities that carry between levels.

📋

Annotation Tools

Mark cards with stamps (innocent, suspect, unknown) and color ribbons to track your deductions as clues build up.

🎓

Interactive Tutorial

A guided 15-step tutorial walks you through every mechanic with a fixed scenario. Master the basics before facing the real council.
